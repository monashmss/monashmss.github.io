A place for the musicians of Monash...

The Monash Music Students’ Society is a club established by students of the university’s music faculty, primarily aimed at enhancing cohesion amongst students, facilitating communication between staff and students, providing academic and study support, and holding social events both in and outside of the university. After a year of planning, MMSS was officially inaugurated in May 2015 and, in just a few short months, has over 150 members.

Advertise with us...

If you're interested in advertising an upcoming concert, gig, or just generally advertising yourself as a musician, you can send us promotional material and we can give you exposure on our front page. Advertising is limited to MMSS members and costs $5 for continuous advertising for all your upcoming musical events.

Instructions

Send an email to music@monashclubs.org with the following details:
Subject: Web Promotion
Name of group/band/artist
Name of event/concert/gig (if relevant)
Details of event (if relevant)
A very short blurb (50 words max) about the event or about the artist/s (if wanted)
A promotional image of either the event or the artist/s
Four availabilities to arrange a physical meeting with one of our committee members in order to pay for advertising.

Image Requirements

File type: JPEG
Dimensions: 1000px x 1000px
Quality: 'Save for Web'
If you do not have the ability to 'Save for Web', please submit a high resolution file of any format (1000px x 1000px) and the file will be converted to low resolution.

Sir Zelman Cowen School of Music


The Sir Zelman Cowen School of Music strives to inspire musicians towards excellence that knows no borders. In scholarship, composition and performance our aim is to develop the musician of tomorrow through a progressive, holistic and culturally diverse approach. The School excels in classical, jazz and popular music performance, its internationally renowned ethnomusicology and musicology programs, and vibrant programs in composition, improvisation, and music technology. The School offers overseas study in New York and Prato (Italy), and works in close collaboration with the Monash Academy of Performing Arts (MAPA) to provide extraordinary performance opportunities. Our graduates are intellectually informed, creatively equipped and professionally geared to engage with the musical challenges of the globalised world of the 21st century.

Monash Clubs & Societies


Over 100 student-run Clubs & Societies run activities, events, competitions, productions, get-togethers, seminars, camps, conferences and more for over 10,000 student members. Clubs & Societies aims to foster student participation in clubs, facilitate the activities and administration of clubs, provide financial and administrative support to clubs and liaise between clubs, the Monash Student Association and the University.
