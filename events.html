Jazz Theory Tutorials

Wednesdays, Semester 2, 2015; 12:00pm-1:00pm
Room 213

Our very first series of Music Theory tutorials to support ATS 1900 & ATS2902. In these session, Paul Cornelius will be talking about various topics covered in the Jazz Music Theory unit. Each stream has a program of 4 sessions and the material covered is progressive. Please note that content is not being repeated. However, questions about any theory-related topics are welcome at all tutorials.
No registration needed, just rock up!

PLEASE NOTE: These programs are strictly NOT to replace official Monash University classes and are solely an extra resource for students who are members of the Monash Music Students’ Society.
